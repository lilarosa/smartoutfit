# Smart Outfit Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-05-25

This draft is for product development and App Store preparation. It should be reviewed before public launch.

## Product Positioning

Smart Outfit is a private wardrobe assistant. The app helps users organize clothing, create outfit ideas, record outfit history, manage care reminders, and optionally keep lightweight lifestyle notes.

Smart Outfit does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services.

## Data We Collect

Data is collected only when users choose to enter or upload it.

- Clothing photos and uploaded wardrobe images.
- Clothing details such as category, color, season, occasion, material, care notes, and purchase date.
- Outfit records, favorite outfits, and travel packing lists.
- Optional lifestyle records such as weight, menstrual cycle notes, mood, and important events.
- Technical information needed to operate the app, such as upload metadata and basic diagnostics.

## How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Display and organize the user's private wardrobe.
- Generate outfit recommendations.
- Help classify clothing and prepare import drafts.
- Show care, idle clothing, color, and packing suggestions.
- Display optional cycle and mood reminders based on user-entered records.

## Photos and AI

When image recognition is enabled, uploaded clothing photos may be processed to identify clothing category, color, season, occasion, and care hints. Images are used for the user's own wardrobe features only.

Smart Outfit should not use private user photos for advertising, public profiles, or model training unless the user has explicitly agreed.

## Health and Lifestyle Records

Weight, menstrual cycle, ovulation estimates, mood, and event notes are optional lifestyle records. Reminders are estimates based on user-entered data and are not medical advice.

Users should consult qualified professionals for medical questions.

## Sharing and Public Visibility

Smart Outfit is private by default.

The app does not include a public community, public user profiles, comments, likes, followers, or public wardrobe browsing in the launch version.

## Deletion

Users should be able to delete:

- Individual clothing items and their uploaded images.
- All wardrobe data.
- Health and lifestyle records.
- Outfit calendar entries.
- Favorite outfits.
- Travel packing lists.
- Their account and associated data if accounts are added.

## Data Export

The product should support data export before public launch or shortly after launch if account sync is introduced.
